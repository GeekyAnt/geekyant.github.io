MiniDSP Control – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

MiniDSP Control does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers or third parties.

How It Works

The app communicates exclusively with a user-operated local control server running on the users local area network. That server acts as a control gateway to a MiniDSP Flex HTx audio processor. All communication stays entirely within your local network.

Local Network Access

Local network access is required solely to connect to your Raspberry Pi control server. No data is transmitted outside your local network.

Data Storage

The only data stored on your device is the IP address of your local control server and any custom configuration profiles you choose to save, stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults.

Third Parties

This app does not integrate any third party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
